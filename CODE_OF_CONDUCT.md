# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free project community for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level, or perspective.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, while being precise about risks.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Protect private information and never request or disclose real secrets, tenant data, mailbox data, or proprietary details.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Sharing real credentials, tenant identifiers, mailbox addresses, logs, or proprietary operational data.
- Encouraging unsafe deployment practices.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent participation restrictions.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through the project owner's designated contact channel. Do not include private third-party data unless it is necessary for the report.
